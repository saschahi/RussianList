Project-T Shop Addon

Project-T Shop Addon is an Addon to Project-T made by Saschahi to give viewers the ability to Buy NPCs and Items in Streamers Playthroughs.

  • !bal - check yourbalance
  • !buy - Syntax: !buy zombie 6 - buy an NPC or Item
  • !purchaselist - Brings you here
  • BEWARE! Some NPCs can only be spawned under certain conditions!
  • If it confirms your Purchase but the monster didn't appear, the conditions haven't been met!
